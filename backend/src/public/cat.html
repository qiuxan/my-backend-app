About This Cat

This adorable feline friend brings joy and warmth to our hearts. Cats are wonderful companions known for their independence, playfulness, and affectionate nature. They have been beloved pets for thousands of years and continue to charm us with their unique personalities.

Cat Facts:

  • Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees
  • A group of cats is called a "clowder"
  • Cats spend 70% of their lives sleeping
  • A cat's purr vibrates at a frequency that promotes healing
  • Cats have a third eyelid called a "nictitating membrane"

Whether they're chasing a laser pointer, curled up in a sunny spot, or showing affection with gentle head bumps, cats have a special way of making every day brighter.
